python 2.2+ can handle long integer.  if you type

>>> 1000000000000000000000000


then you will get
1000000000000000000000000L

you may noticed "L" at the end to identify the Long Integer value.

so, you can do something like

>>> 592838753984729847L + 2395792834579238483964L + 234
2396385673333223214045L
